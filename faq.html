Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and more.

We use only premium materials for our sofas. Our frames are constructed from kiln-dried hardwood for maximum durability. Upholstery options include top-grain Italian leather, performance velvet, and high-quality linen blends. Cushions feature high-resiliency foam wrapped in down or down-alternative for ultimate comfort.

In-stock items typically ship within 3-5 business days and arrive within 1-2 weeks. Custom orders or out-of-stock items usually take 6-8 weeks to be crafted and delivered. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped.

Yes! We offer white-glove delivery for an additional fee. This service includes delivery to your room of choice, unpacking, full assembly, and removal of all packaging materials. You can select this option during checkout.

We offer a 30-day return policy on all standard items. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, minus return shipping costs. Custom orders are non-refundable unless there is a manufacturing defect.
